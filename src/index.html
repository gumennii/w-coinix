Our Solutions

Our Solutions

  • Asset-based Lending

    Access liquidity without selling your bitcoin. Need a boost for investments or urgent projects? Borrow cash against your crypto holdings. Competitive rates with interest payable in kind.

  • Portfolio Management

    Place your assets in thoughtfully constructed, professionally managed investments which allow visibility to off-market opportunities.

  • Liquidity Services

    Coinix offers liquidity services to help clients meet commitments to exchanges and other liquidity venues. Market Making solutions facilitate trades by ensuring there is a counterparty, helping to reduce price volatility and provide essential market liquidity.

  • Placement Services

    We have built up an extensive network of professional and institutional investors since establishment. Clients that choose our placement services benefit from the secure, compliant and efficient distribution of crypto assets to these investors.

  • OTC - Exchange Services

    Coinix OTC services provide clients a flexible and secure solution for executing digital asset trades. Enjoy personalized service, and access to an extensive network of liquidity providers with our OTC services.

  • On-Off Ramp Services

    Coinix on-off ramp services enable clients to convert between fiat currency and digital assets. Coinix offers a flexible and secure on-ramp (EUR to crypto) and off-ramp (crypto to EUR) solutions, ensuring compliance and competitive rates. Simplify your entry and exit from the digital asset market with our trusted services.
